For You 💌

Dear Didiiiii,

Today you turn 21, and somehow that feels unreal... because in my heart, you are still the same person who has always been my strength, my comfort, and my quiet sense of home.

We are both away from home now. Away from our parents, away from the familiarity we grew up with, and away from each other too... chasing our studies, our dreams, and the future we hope to build.

I know this isn’t easy. Especially when being strong feels heavier than it should. For our parents, this distance is difficult. And for you, I know it takes courage every single day. On your 21st birthday, I wish you even more strength... the kind that doesn’t shout, but quietly keeps going.

I miss you. I miss our late-night conversations, the random talks that stretched into silence, and the comfort of knowing you were just there, even without saying much.

I miss the small things too... the random hugs I would give you for no reason at all, just to annoy you, just to steal a moment of comfort. I miss teasing you endlessly, watching you pretend to be irritated, while knowing you always let me... because that’s how we’ve always been: a little chaos, and a lot of love.

Even though I miss you deeply, I understand why this distance exists. Not because we want to be apart, but because both of us deserve the dreams we are working so hard for. And no matter how far we are, this little distance can never weaken our bond.

I love you.. more than I say, more than I show, and probably more than you realise. I will always be thankful for your love, for how protective you have been of me since childhood, for standing up for me, and for caring in ways that always made me feel safe.

Happy 21st Birthday, Didi. May this year give you courage when things feel hard, peace when things feel overwhelming, and the reminder that you are never alone... not today, not ever.

With all my love,
your Khumshii 🤍
