Things I Admire

Things I may not say often,
but notice every day.

Your quiet strength The way you always protect me Your patience (even when I test it) How you stay calm for everyone else Your honesty The love you give without asking for anything back

You’ve always carried more than you show... responsibilities, expectations, worries and yet you still choose to carry all along with you.

I hope you know how deeply that is admired.
